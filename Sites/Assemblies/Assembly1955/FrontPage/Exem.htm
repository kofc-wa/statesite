I would request that any assembly or council requiring a waiver (i.e., for less than one year as a Knight) contact me at the above email address or telephone number. In order to grant a waiver request, the candidate must have had his 1st degree at least six months prior, must have received his second and third degree, and must submit the Form 4 along with his $65.00.  Information required for the waiver is the candidate’s name, council number, first degree date, membership number, and number of the Assembly that he will join.

 

A member with less than 6 months since his first degree can only be granted a waiver to join the Fourth Degree if he is a member of the military (e.g., with a pending overseas or out or state assignment), is a member of the clergy, or has a significant medical condition that causes time to be of the essence.  Contact me to discuss any candidate in need of this type of waiver.

 

I would ask that this information receive the largest dissemination possible. It is imperative that Form 4’s be forwarded to me upon receipt.  If there are any questions about any of the times or where the degree will be held, call me or email me.
