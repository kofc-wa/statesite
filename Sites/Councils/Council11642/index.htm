The Fr. John A. Walsh Council meets the Parish Learning Center at St. Monica Church in Mercer Island, Washington.  St. Monica’s address is 4301 88th Avenue SE. The Grand Knight for 2011-2012 is Terry Welsh. You can contact the Knights by phone by calling Todd Elliott at (425) 281-0051 or Terry Welsh at (425) 204-5518.


Business Meetings are generally held on the third Monday of the month in the Parish Learning Center at 7:30 PM.  Please consult the calendar on the Activities page.


Fr. John A. Walsh Council was founded in 1995 through the cooperation and approval of Fr. John Bowman, then pastor of St. Monica Parish and the strident efforts of the first Grand Knight Leo Zipp.  The Council was named after the beloved and much revered Fr. John A. Walsh, the founding Pastor of St. Monica Parish in 1958.  Father Walsh was born in County Sligo, Ireland and was ordained in 1946.  He served as the pastor of St. Monica for more than eighteen years.  Father Walsh died of a heart attack shortly after being reassigned in 1977 at age 56.  The Fr. John A. Walsh Council works closely with St. Monica Parish by providing funds for various projects, sponsoring activities for the parishioners and otherwise being active participants in the parish.
