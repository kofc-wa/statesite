Fr. John A. Walsh Council 11642 
   •   Mercer Island, Washington   •   

  



 
Business meetings are held on the 3rd Monday of the month (September through June) at 7:30 PM in the Parish Learning Center.

Our council hosts a large number of activities throughout the year.  Activities benefit our Church, our Community, our Families, our Youth and our Council.  These activities include support of the Parish Picnic and Auction, hosting coffee and donuts after the Sunday masses, serving an annual Italian Dinner, serving several Breakfast Buffets, co-hosting a Symposium on Life Issues, sponsorship of monthly lecture series on spiritual topics of interest to the Parish community, providing rosaries to First Communicants, collecting funds for service men and women in local area hospitals, providing support for seminarians, judging an essay contest for St. Monica eighth graders, running a free-throw contest for youths, collecting food for the needy and many other rewarding activities.
