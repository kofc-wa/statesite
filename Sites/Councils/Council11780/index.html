Council Information
Harry J Tucker Jr. Council is part of St John the Baptist Parish.
The parish is located at 25810 156th Avenue S.E. in Covington, WA.
The Grand Knight for 2010-2011 is Scott Gilyeat.

Council meetings
Regular General Meetings are held on the first Tuesday, at 7:00 PM in the McMullen Education Center.
Our Business Meetings are held on the third Tuesday, at 7:00 PM in the McMullen Education Center.

About our Council
The Harry J. Tucker Jr. Council 11780 was instituted May 5, 1996.
