About Our Council....

Holy Spirit Council #10653 is affiliated with the Catholic Parish of the Holy Spirit located at 7409 W. Clearwater Avenue in Kennewick, WA. Our Grand Knight for 2007-2008 is Stan Hicks. You can contact our Council by email at stan-jo@charter.net.

Our Council, which was founded in 1991, has members who are active participants in various Parish ministries, boards, and functions and who are dedicated to providing resources and support for a variety of Church, Family, Youth, Community, and Pro-life projects.

We are also spiritually and financially committed to religious vocations by our direct support to men and women who desire to seek and serve the Priesthood, Deaconate, Brotherhood, and Sisterhood through our Refund Support for Vocations Program (RSVP) and "Pennies for Heaven." In 2006, the Pennies for Heaven program provided almost $63,000 to the three bishops of Washington state for seminarian aid.

Business meetings are held at the Catholic Parish of the Holy Spirit on the second and fourth Tuesday of each month at 7:30 pm..
