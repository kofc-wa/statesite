Our council hosts a large number of council activities throughout the year.

Some of our annual activities include: Council open house, Christmas Party, Altar Server Appreciation Awards & Pizza Feed, Scholarship breakfast

We have activities in each of the following areas: Church Activities, Youth Activities, Family Activities, Community Service and Council Activities.
