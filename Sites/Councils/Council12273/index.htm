The Knights of the Colville Council hold their meetings at the Immaculate Conception Catholic Church in Colville (see address above). The Grand Knight for 2006-2007 is Ed Broadhurst. Ed Broadhurst can be reached at (509) 738-4949, or by email at edb@centurytel.net Or you can contact our membership chairman, Ed Parazoo, by phone at (509) 738-4515, or by email at eparazoo@plix.com.
Our normal business meeting is held on the 2nd Thursday of each month at 7:00 PM. These meetings are held at the parish hall on the Immaculate Conception Catholic Church campus.


The Knights of the Colville Council was founded in the fall of 1998 through the efforts of Ed Parazoo and Rick Kubesh. The Knights of the Colville Council works closely with the Immaculate Conception Parish in Colville and Sacred Heart of Jesus Parish in Kettle Falls, organizing and running fundraisers for various charities in the Parishes and Communities, and extending a helping hand in a variety of volunteer projects, and being active participants in their parish.
